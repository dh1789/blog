## 5. Cookie Usage

### 5.1 What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store in users' browsers. Cookies are used to improve user experience and understand website usage.

### 5.2 Types of Cookies Used

- **Essential Cookies**: Provide basic website functionality (session management, etc.)
- **Analytics Cookies**: Website usage analysis through Google Analytics, etc.
- **Advertising Cookies**: Personalized advertising through Google AdSense
- **Functional Cookies**: Remember user settings (language, theme, etc.)

### 5.3 Cookie Management

Most browsers provide settings to manage cookies:
- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data

Rejecting cookies may limit some functionality.
